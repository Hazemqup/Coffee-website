Our History & Our Mission

Discover our History and the Mission of Mug & Mingle

Our History

Mug & Mingle was founded in 2013 with the straightforward goal of establishing a location where coffee lovers could congregate chosen coffee experiences. We started out as a tiny neighbourhood establishment and have since expanded into a coffee shop that is focused on the community.

Our Mission

Our goal is to create a sense of community and connection among our customers while providing them with ethically sourced, premium coffe from across the globe. We are dedicated to innovation and sustainability, making sure that each cup we serve makes the world a better place for the one who drinks from our coffe.
